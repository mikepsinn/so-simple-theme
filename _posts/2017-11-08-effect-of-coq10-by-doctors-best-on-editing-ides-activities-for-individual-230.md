This individual's  data suggests with a high degree of confidence (p=1.2174733839899E-8, 95% CI -0.081 to 0.435) that CoQ10 By Doctors Best has a weakly positive predictive relationship (R=0.18) with Editing &amp; IDEs Activities. The highest quartile of Editing &amp; IDEs Activities measurements were observed following an average 380.99 milligrams CoQ10 By Doctors Best per day. The lowest quartile of Editing &amp; IDEs Activities measurements were observed following an average 245.09090909091 mg CoQ10 By Doctors Best per day.Editing &amp; IDEs Activities is generally 13% lower than normal after a total of 245.09090909091 milligrams of CoQ10 By Doctors Best intake over the previous 7 days.
